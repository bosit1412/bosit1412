Mining and Resource Depletion:

The production of IT infrastructure relies heavily on the mining of rare earth minerals, such as cobalt and coltan. These minerals are essential for the production of various electronic devices and present several environmental challenges. Mining activities result in the destruction of natural habitats, deforestation, and soil degradation. Additionally, the extraction and processing of these minerals consume vast amounts of water, contributing to water scarcity in many regions.
